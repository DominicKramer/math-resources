Using the formula for the determinant of a $2\times 2$ matrix given in \acronymref{theorem}{DMST}, we have 
%
\begin{align*}
\det{(B)} &= 
\begin{vmatrix} 
2 - x & 1\\ 
4 & 2-x 
\end{vmatrix} 
= (2 - x)(2 - x) - 4 = x^2 - 4x = x(x-4)
\end{align*}
%
and thus $\det{(B)} = 0$ only when $x=0$ or $x=4$.
