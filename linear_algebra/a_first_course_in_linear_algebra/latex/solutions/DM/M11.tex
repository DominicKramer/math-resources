\begin{align*}
\detname{A} &= 
\begin{vmatrix} 
1 & 2 & 1 \\ 
2 & 0 & 1 \\ 
2& 3 & k 
\end{vmatrix} 
= 7 - 4k
\end{align*}
Thus, $\detname{A} = 0$ only when $k = \frac{7}{4}$.
