The row reduced form of matrix $A$ is 
$\begin{bmatrix} 
\leading{1} & 0 & -1 & 3 & 5\\ 
0 & \leading{1} & 1& -1 & 3\\ 
0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0
\end{bmatrix}$, 
so the rank of $A$ (number of columns with leading 1's) is $2$, and the nullity is $5 - 2 = 3$.
