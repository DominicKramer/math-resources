\begin{align*}
\vect{y}\in\rsp{A}
&\iff\vect{y}\in\csp{\transpose{A}}&&\text{\acronymref{definition}{RSM}}\\
&\iff\linearsystem{\transpose{A}}{\vect{y}}\text{ is consistent}&&\text{\acronymref{theorem}{CSCS}}
\end{align*}
%
The augmented matrix $\augmented{\transpose{A}}{\vect{y}}$ row reduces to
%
\begin{align*}
\begin{bmatrix}
 \leading{1} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & \leading{1} & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & \leading{1} & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & \leading{1}
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
%
and with a leading 1 in the final column \acronymref{theorem}{RCLS} tells us the linear system is inconsistent and so $\vect{y}\not\in\rsp{A}$.
