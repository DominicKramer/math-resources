The coefficient matrix and vector of constants for the system are
%
\begin{align*}
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 & 3 & 1\\ 
-2 & -1 & -4 & -1\\ 
1 & 4 & 10 & 2\\ 
-2 & 0 & -4 & 5
\end{bmatrix}&&
\vect{b}=\colvector{5\\-7\\9\\9}
\end{align*}
%
$\inverse{A}$ can be computed by using a calculator, or by the method of \acronymref{theorem}{CINM}.
Then \acronymref{theorem}{SNCM} says the unique solution is
%
\begin{equation*}
\inverse{A}\vect{b}=
\begin{bmatrix}
38 & 18 & -5 & -2\\ 
96 & 47 & -12 & -5\\ 
-39 & -19 & 5 & 2\\ 
-16 & -8 & 2 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
\colvector{5\\-7\\9\\9}
=
\colvector{1\\-2\\1\\3}
%
\end{equation*}
