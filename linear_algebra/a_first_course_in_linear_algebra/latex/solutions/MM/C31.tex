$A^2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -2\\ 0 & 1\end{bmatrix}$, 
$A^3 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -3\\ 0 & 1\end{bmatrix}$, 
$A^4 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -4\\ 0 & 1\end{bmatrix}$.  
From this pattern, we see that 
$A^n = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -n\\ 0 & 1\end{bmatrix}$.
