Prove that Hermitian matrices (\acronymref{definition}{HM}) have real entries on the diagonal.  More precisely, suppose that $A$ is a Hermitian matrix of size $n$.  Then $\matrixentry{A}{ii}\in\real{\null}$, $1\leq i\leq n$.
