$A = 
\begin{bmatrix}  
1 & 2 & 1 & 1 \\ 
2 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\ 
1 & 2 & 1 & 2 \\ 
1 & 2 & 1 & 1 
\end{bmatrix}$ 
and let 
$\ltdefn{T}{\complex{4}}{\complex{4}}$ be given by 
$\lt{T}{\vect{x}}=A\vect{x}$.  
Find $\krn{T}$.
