For the matrix $A$ below, find a linearly independent set $S$ so that the null space of $A$ is spanned by $S$, that is, $\nsp{A}=\spn{S}$. 
%
\begin{equation*}
A=
\begin{bmatrix}
 -1 & -2 & 2 & 1 & 5 \\
 1 & 2 & 1 & 1 & 5 \\
 3 & 6 & 1 & 2 & 7 \\
 2 & 4 & 0 & 1 & 2
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
