Find a set of column vectors, $T$, such that (1) the span of $T$ is the null space of $B$, $\spn{T}=\nsp{B}$ and (2) $T$ is a linearly independent set.
%
\begin{align*}
B&=
\begin{bmatrix}
 2 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\
 -4 & -3 & 1 & -7 \\
 1 & 1 & -1 & 3
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
