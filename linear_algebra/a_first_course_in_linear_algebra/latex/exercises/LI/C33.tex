Find  a set $S$ so that $S$ is linearly independent and $\nsp{A}=\spn{S}$, where $\nsp{A}$ is the null space of the matrix $A$ below.
%
\begin{align*}
A&=
\begin{bmatrix}
2 & 3 & 3 & 1 & 4 \\
1 & 1 & -1 & -1 & -3 \\
3 & 2 & -8 & -1 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
