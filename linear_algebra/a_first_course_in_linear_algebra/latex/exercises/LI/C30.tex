For the matrix $B$ below, find a set $S$ that is linearly independent and spans the null space of $B$, that is, $\nsp{B}=\spn{S}$.
%
\begin{equation*}
B=
\begin{bmatrix}
-3 & 1 & -2 & 7\\ 
-1 & 2 & 1 & 4\\ 
1 & 1 & 2 & -1
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
