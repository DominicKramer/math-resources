Suppose that $\vect{u},\,\vect{v},\,\vect{w}\in\complex{n}$, $\alpha,\,\beta\in\complexes$ and $\vect{u}$ is orthogonal to both $\vect{v}$ and $\vect{w}$.  Prove that $\vect{u}$ is orthogonal to $\alpha\vect{v}+\beta\vect{w}$.
