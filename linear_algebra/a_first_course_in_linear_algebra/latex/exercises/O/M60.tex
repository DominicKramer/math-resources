Suppose that $\set{\vect{u},\,\vect{v},\,\vect{w}}\subseteq\complex{n}$ is an orthonormal set.  Prove that $\vect{u}+\vect{v}$ is not orthogonal to $\vect{v}+\vect{w}$.
