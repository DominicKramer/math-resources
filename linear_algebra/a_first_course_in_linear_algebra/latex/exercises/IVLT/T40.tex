Suppose $\ltdefn{T}{U}{V}$ and $\ltdefn{S}{V}{W}$ are linear transformations and $\dimension{U}=\dimension{V}=\dimension{W}$.  Suppose that $\compose{S}{T}$ is invertible.  Prove that $S$ and $T$ are individually invertible (this could be construed as a converse of \acronymref{theorem}{CIVLT}).
