Determine if the linear transformation $\ltdefn{S}{P_3}{M_{22}}$ is (a) injective, (b) surjective, (c) invertible.
\begin{equation*}
\lt{S}{a+bx+cx^2+dx^3}=
\begin{bmatrix}
-a+4b+c+2d & 4a-b+6c-d\\
a+5b-2c+2d & a+2c+5d
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
