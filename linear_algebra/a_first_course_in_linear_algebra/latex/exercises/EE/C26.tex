For matrix 
$A = \begin{bmatrix} 
2 & 1 & 1\\
1 & 2 & 1\\
1 & 1 & 2 
\end{bmatrix}$, the characteristic polynomial of $A$ is $\charpoly{A}{\lambda} = (4-x)(1-x)^2$.  
Find the eigenvalues and corresponding eigenspaces of $A$.
