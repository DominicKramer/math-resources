Construct a new solution to \acronymref{exercise}{B.T50} along the following outline.  From the $n\times n$ matrix $A$, construct the linear transformation $\ltdefn{T}{\complex{n}}{\complex{n}}$, $\lt{T}{\vect{x}}=A\vect{x}$.  Use \acronymref{theorem}{NI}, \acronymref{theorem}{IMILT} and \acronymref{theorem}{ILTIS} to translate between the nonsingularity of $A$ and the surjectivity/injectivity of $T$.  Then apply \acronymref{theorem}{ILTB} and  \acronymref{theorem}{SLTB} to connect these properties with bases.
