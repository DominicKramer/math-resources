Let $T$ be the set of columns of the matrix $B$ below.  Define $W=\spn{T}$.  Find a set $R$ so that (1) $R$ has 3 vectors, (2) $R$ is a subset of $T$, and (3) $W=\spn{R}$.
%
\begin{equation*}
B=
\begin{bmatrix}
-3 & 1 & -2 & 7\\ 
-1 & 2 & 1 & 4\\ 
1 & 1 & 2 & -1
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
