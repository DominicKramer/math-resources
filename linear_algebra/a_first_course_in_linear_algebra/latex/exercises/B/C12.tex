Find a basis for the vector space $T$ of lower triangular $3 \times 3$ matrices;  
that is, matrices of the form 
$\begin{bmatrix} * & 0 & 0\\ * & * & 0\\ * & * & *\end{bmatrix}$ 
where an asterisk represents any complex number.
