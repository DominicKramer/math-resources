Verify that $B$ is the inverse of $A$.
%
\begin{align*}
A&=
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 & -1 & 2\\
-2 & -1 & 2 & -3\\
1 & 1 & 0 & 2\\
-1 & 2 & 0 & 2
\end{bmatrix}
&
B&=
\begin{bmatrix}
4 & 2 & 0 & -1\\
8 & 4 & -1 & -1\\
-1 & 0 & 1 & 0\\
-6 & -3 & 1 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
