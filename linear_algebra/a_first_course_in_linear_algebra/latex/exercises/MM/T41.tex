Suppose that $A$ is an $n\times n$ nonsingular matrix and $B$ is an $n\times p$ matrix.  Prove that the null space of $B$ is equal to the null space of $AB$, that is $\nsp{B}=\nsp{AB}$.  (Compare with \acronymref{exercise}{MM.T40}.)
