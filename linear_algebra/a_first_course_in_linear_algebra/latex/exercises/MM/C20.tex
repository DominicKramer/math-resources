Compute the product of the two matrices below, $AB$.  Do this using the definitions of the matrix-vector product (\acronymref{definition}{MVP}) and the definition of matrix multiplication (\acronymref{definition}{MM}).
%
\begin{align*}
A=
\begin{bmatrix}
2&5\\
-1&3\\
2&-2
\end{bmatrix}
&&
B=\begin{bmatrix}
1&5&-3&4\\
2&0&2&-3
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
