The following archetypes are systems of equations.  For each different coefficient matrix build two new vectors of constants.  The first should lead to a consistent system and the second should lead to an inconsistent system.  Descriptions of the column space as spans of linearly independent sets of vectors with ``nice patterns'' of zeros and ones might be most useful and instructive in connection with this exercise.
