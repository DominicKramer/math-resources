Suppose that $S=\set{
\colvector{-1 \\ 2 \\ 1},\,
\colvector{ 3 \\ 1 \\ 2},\,
\colvector{ 1 \\ 5 \\ 4},\,
\colvector{-6 \\ 5 \\ 1}
}$. Let $W=\spn{S}$ and let $\vect{y}=\colvector{-5\\3\\0}$.  Is $\vect{y}\in W$?  If so, provide an explicit linear combination that demonstrates this.
