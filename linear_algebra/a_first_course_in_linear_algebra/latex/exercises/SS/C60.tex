For the matrix $A$ below, find a set of vectors $S$ so that the span of $S$ equals the null space of $A$, $\spn{S}=\nsp{A}$.
%
\begin{equation*}
A=
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 & 6 & -8\\
1 & -2 & 0 & 1\\
-2 & 1 & -6 & 7
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
