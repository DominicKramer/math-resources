Suppose that $A$ is an $m\times n$ matrix.  Define the linear transformation $T$ by
%
\begin{equation*}
\ltdefn{T}{\complex{n}}{\complex{m}},\quad \lt{T}{\vect{x}}=A\vect{x}
\end{equation*}
%
Prove that the range of $T$ equals the column space of $A$, $\rng{T}=\csp{A}$.
