Given the matrix 
$B = 
\begin{bmatrix} 
4 - x & -4 & -4\\ 
2 & -2 - x & -4\\ 
3 & -3 & -4 -x
\end{bmatrix}$, 
find all values of $x$ that are solutions of $\det(B) = 0$.
