Find a value of $k$ so that the matrix 
$A = \begin{bmatrix} 
1 & 2 & 1\\
2 & 0 & 1 \\
2 & 3 & k
\end{bmatrix}$ 
has $\det(A) = 0$, or explain why it is not possible.
