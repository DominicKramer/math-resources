Consider the subspace 
%
\begin{equation*}
W=\spn{\set{
\begin{bmatrix}
2 & 1\\3 & -1
\end{bmatrix}
,\,
\begin{bmatrix}
4 & 0\\2 & 3
\end{bmatrix}
,\,
\begin{bmatrix}
-3 & 1\\2 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
}}
\end{equation*}
%
of the vector space of $2\times 2$ matrices, $M_{22}$.  Is 
$
C=
\begin{bmatrix}
-3 & 3\\6 & -4
\end{bmatrix}
$
an element of $W$?
