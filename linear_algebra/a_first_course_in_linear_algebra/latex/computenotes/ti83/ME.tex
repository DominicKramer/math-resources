On the TI-83, press the \computerfont{MATRX} key.  Press the right arrow key twice so that \computerfont{EDIT} is highlighted.  Move the cursor down so that it is over the desired letter of the matrix and press \computerfont{ENTER}.  For example, let's call our matrix \computerfont{B}, so press the down arrow once and press \computerfont{ENTER}.  To enter a $2\times 3$ matrix, press \computerfont{2 ENTER 3 ENTER}.  To create the matrix
%
\begin{equation*}
\begin{bmatrix}
1&2&3\\4&5&6
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
%
press \computerfont{1 ENTER 2 ENTER 3 ENTER 4 ENTER 5 ENTER 6 ENTER}.
